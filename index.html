MEET THE TEAM

Dr. Taylor is a member of the Academy of General Dentistry, the American Dental Association, the Florida Dental Association, and the Alachua County Dental Association.

Cathy Taylor, DMD

Dr. Cathy Taylor was born and raised in sunny Sarasota, Florida just a couple of hours south of Gainesville. She graduated from the University of Florida with a degree in Nutritional Science and was a Gator Cheerleader during her undergraduate studies, along with her twin sister, Becky! She continued her education at UF's College of Dentistry. While in dental school, Dr. Taylor was a member of the American Student Dental Association, Dental Ambassadors, Project H.E.A.L., an inderdisciplinary trip to Quito, Ecuador to provide dental care, and was president of the Christian Dental Society.

Following dental school, Dr. Taylor chose to further her education by participating in an Advanced Education in General Dentistry program based in Apopka, Florida. It was during this program that Dr. Taylor decided she wanted to make Gainesville her permanent home. She is a member of the Alachua County Dental Association, the Academy of General Dentistry, and the American Dental Association.

Dr. Taylor and her husband, Andrew, met while students at UF and married shortly after graduation. Andrew, a Certified Public Accountant, works with Dr. Taylor at Taylor Dentistry while also maintaining an account businesses.

The passion and joy of their lives are their two daughters, Carlee Jay, 6, and Ellie, 3. They are blessed with family living nearby and spend much of their non-working hours sharing time with grandparents, aunts, uncles, and cousins.

Connie Aledter

Connie and Dr. Taylor have had the pleasure of working together since Dr. Taylor's opening day of her practice.

Connie was born, raised and educated in Germany and moved to Gainesville in 2001 where she lives with her two daughters and grandson.

She has over 29 years experience in the dental field helping patients navigate their insurance benefits and finding ways for all patients to afford optimal dental care.

In her spare time, Connie enjoys spending time with friends and loves watching the Gators play.

Connie is gifted in having an eye for design! She helps keep Taylor Dentistry looking fresh and modern to create an inviting and welcoming environment for our patients.

Trevor Crovitz

Trevor comes to Taylor Dentistry with a wealth of experience in the dental field. He has worked over 20 years as an assistant, office manage, and instructor.

His altruism began in 1990 when he enlisted in the US Air Force and served for four years. After his tour, he attended Flagler College where he was a member of the men's basketball team. In 1999, he joined the US Army National Guard which later included a tour in Iraq for a year. Upon completion of his time in the Army, Trevor earned his sociology degree at Kean University.

An avid sports fan, Trevor also writes for a sports blog. He currently resides in Gainesville with his girlfriend, daughter, dog and super cute cat!

Cara Barfield

Cara, born in Orlando and raised in Tampa, is a Florida girl through and through. After graduating high school, she attended a culinary school in Miami where she quickly discovered cooking in her own kitchen is more satisfying. In 2009, Cara moved to Gainesville where she attended Santa Fe College, graduating with her A.S. in Dental Hygiene.

In her spare time, Cara enjoys spending quality time with friends, going to concerts, and trying out new restaurants in town. She frequently travels to Tampa and Orlando to spend time with her family. Cara likes to live a healthy lifestyle by regularly going to the gym and cooking well at home, but she definitely has a sweet tooth that she keeps in check with routine dental care.

Cara experiences great joy with educating patients on the importance of a healthy mouth and strives to make everyone's visit to Taylor Dentistry comfortable and stress-free.
